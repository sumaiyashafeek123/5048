About Me

I'm Sumaiya Shafeek and I'm professionally a Photographer

I have always had a passion for capturing moments and telling stories through my images. I specialize in street and architechtural photography. And I love to work with clients to bring their vision to life. I have 6 years of experience in the field, and have had the pleasure of working with a variety of clients in different settings. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new places and finding inspiration for my next shoot.


I'm excited to connect with other photographers and collaborate on future projects.As a photographer, my goal is to deliver images that my client will love and through that build a strong relationship with my clients. And get others to recognize or acknowledge the positive qualities of my photographs.
